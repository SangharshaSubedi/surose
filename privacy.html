Privacy Policy

BAL BISHNU Co., Ltd.  ·  Established May 1, 2008

BAL BISHNU Co., Ltd. (hereinafter "the Company"), the operator of SUNROSE Indian Restaurant, recognizes the critical importance of protecting the personal information of our customers and all individuals who interact with our services. We are fully committed to complying with the Act on the Protection of Personal Information (個人情報保護法) and all other applicable laws and regulations in Japan. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, manage, and protect your personal information.

1 Collection of Personal Information

The Company collects personal information — including but not limited to your name, telephone number, email address, and the number of guests in your party — only when it is necessary for the purpose of accepting reservations, providing restaurant services, responding to inquiries, or improving the quality of our services. We collect information through means that are lawful, fair, and transparent, and only with the knowledge and consent of the individual concerned.

2 Purpose of Use

Personal information collected by the Company is used solely for the following purposes: processing and confirming reservations; communicating with guests regarding their visit; responding to inquiries or feedback; and improving our services and customer experience. We will not use your personal information for any purpose beyond what is stated above without your prior consent.

3 Management & Security

The Company takes appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect personal information from unauthorised access, loss, destruction, falsification, or leakage. Access to personal information is strictly limited to employees who require it to fulfil their duties. All staff members are trained in and obligated to uphold the confidentiality of customer data. We regularly review and strengthen our security practices to ensure your information remains safe.

4 Third-Party Disclosure

The Company will not disclose, sell, or provide personal information to any third party without the prior consent of the individual, except where required by law or regulation, or where disclosure is necessary to protect the life, health, or property of an individual in an urgent situation. In cases where we engage third-party service providers who may handle personal information on our behalf (such as reservation platforms), we ensure they are bound by equivalent data protection obligations.

5 Cookies & Website Data

Our website may use cookies or similar technologies to improve the browsing experience and understand how visitors use our site. These technologies do not identify you personally and are used solely for analytical and functional purposes. You may disable cookies through your browser settings at any time, though this may affect certain features of the website.

6 Your Rights & Amendments

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information held by the Company at any time. Please contact us using the details below and we will respond promptly. The Company reserves the right to amend this Privacy Policy as necessary in response to changes in law or our business practices. Any updates will be published on this page.
