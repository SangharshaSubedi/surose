Our Story

About Us

At SUNROSE, we bring the vibrant soul of Indian cuisine to the heart of Shinagawa. Every dish is crafted with authentic spices sourced directly from India, honoring generations of culinary tradition.

Our warm, inviting space seats up to 80 guests and is perfect for everything from a quiet lunch to a memorable celebration. We are open every day of the year, because great food should never wait.
